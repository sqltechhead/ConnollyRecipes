## Ingredients
- [ ] 150g Brown Sugar
- [ ] 2tbsp Paprika
- [ ] 1tbsp Chili Powder
- [ ] 1tbsp Garlic Powder
- [ ] 1tbsp Onion Powder
- [ ] 1tbsp Salt
- [ ] 1tbsp Ground Black Pepper 

## Method
1. Combine all the ingredients and mix thoroughly.
2. Pour into a spice container, ready to use.
